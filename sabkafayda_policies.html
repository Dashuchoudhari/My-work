Sabka Fayda Policies

Customer Policy

1. Order Confirmation

  • For COD (Cash on Delivery) orders, customers must confirm via call / OTP / app notification.
  • Prepaid orders do not require confirmation.

2. Returns & Replacements

  • Returns are allowed only if the product is damaged, defective, or not as described.
  • Customer must provide an unboxing video for return approval. Returns without video will not be accepted.
  • Replacement is prioritized over refund. Refund is issued only after 3 failed replacement attempts.
  • Non-returnable items: Cosmetics, hygiene products, undergarments, perishable goods.
  • Customers misusing returns (e.g., 3+ times) will get refunds after deducting delivery charges.

3. Product Usage & Condition

  • Products must be returned in original condition with tags, packaging, and seals intact.
  • Products with removed or broken tags will not be eligible for return.
  • Footwear and apparel will include tamper-proof tags or QR-coded seals for fraud prevention.

Seller Policy

1. Packing & Dispatch

  • Sellers must record a packing video for every order.
  • Each shipment must include a tamper-proof seal and barcode/QR code.
  • If the seal is broken before delivery, the courier partner is liable.

2. Shipping & Delivery

  • If a customer refuses delivery, the seller will not bear shipping charges.

3. Return Scenarios

  • If the product is damaged or wrong, the seller bears delivery charges.
  • If the customer rejects the product without valid reason, the seller is safe and no charges apply.
  • Fraudulent returns are prevented via tagging and video proof system.

Summary

  • Customers, sellers, and the platform are all protected from fraud.
  • Replacement is prioritized over refund; refund only after 3 failed attempts.
  • COD orders have confirmation safeguards.
  • Non-returnable items and proper proof prevent misuse and unnecessary losses.
